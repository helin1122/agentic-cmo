# Skill: cmo-engage

## Purpose

Audience engagement and community building. Draft replies, comments, and outreach messages.

## Trigger

Use when the user wants to engage with their audience or target accounts.

## Workflow

1. Read `context/SOCIAL_STRATEGY.md` for target accounts and engagement goals
2. Read `context/VOICE.md` for tone guidelines
3. Draft engagement responses (replies, comments, DMs)
4. Log engagement activity in `outputs/`
5. Update `context/LEARNINGS.md` with engagement insights

## Inputs

- Posts or threads to engage with
- Target accounts or communities
- Engagement goals (visibility, relationship building, etc.)

## Outputs

- Draft replies and comments
- Engagement log in `outputs/`
- Updated learnings
